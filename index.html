Who We Are?

IKAMVA FUTURE LEADERS NPC is a newly formed and registered non-profit organisation whose intention is to give back to the community, primarily the youth of Ward 29 and its surroundings.

It was borne out of an observation by the founders, of the needs of the youth of Mncane and to give hope that a better future is possible despite the background and environment of where one comes from.

The organisation’s main objectives are to:

  • Support underprivileged School Children through projects such as providing them with school uniforms, school shoes, stationery and school fees.
  • Support underprivileged School Children through projects such as providing them with school uniforms, school shoes, stationery and school fees.
  • Skills Development through training courses in disadvantaged areas to empower the communities with skills.

We intend to engage and partner with similar groups, government departments, government and private organisations and keen individuals to achieve these objectives.

Skills Development

Over the coming years we intend to extend our scope from the schools and younger scholars to the young adults and larger communities through the Skills Development objective by offering training courses in the communities thereby equipping them with a variety of skills.

Career Guidance

We plan to also run workshops such as Career Guidance, Work Readiness Workshops for the Youth and the unemployed to prepare them for the workplace.

Community Agriculture

The purpose of this presentation is to engage with our valued stakeholders with the intention to share the dream and the plans Ikamva Future Leaders NPC has for communities in Mncane. We seek our stakeholders’ buy-in and support to achieve our plans starting with the Support of underprivileged School Children

Our initial plans involve a 2-day Sporting Tournament between the identified benefi- ciary schools as well as other surrounding schools with the intention of using the proceeds of those tournaments to support children from the identified beneficiary schools

This tournament will be an Annual Event that will not only serve as a form of entertainment but will also keep the youth off the streets during the December holidays which tends to be an issue out of boredom when they are not at school, killing two birds with one stone.
